About

Hi! My name is Jonah, and I'm a recent university graduate who loves helping students achieve their academic goals. Whether you are trying to get into a specific university program, or are already in post-secondary and just want to improve your marks, I am sure that I can help you get to where you want to be.

My qualifications include a BSc in General Biology from The King's University (4.0 GPA) and a MSc in Data Science from UBCO (4.0 GPA). In the fall, I will be returning to university part-time to obtain my MA in English from the University of Victoria. My teaching experience includes TAing several courses in university, as well as coaching classes at Giants Jiu Jitsu.
