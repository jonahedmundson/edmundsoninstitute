About

The Edmundson Institute is primarily a place for me to publish online courses on a variety of topics. I also offer tutoring services, essay review, and a book club.

Courses

I am in the process of developing a suite of courses in a variety of subjects. They are intended for use in a homeschooling context, but can also be used for self study.

HTML Web Design Course

This course will teach you the basics of web design using HTML and CSS (Javascript is not discussed). Topics include basic elements, responsive design, and web design best practices. By the end of the course, you will have built your own personal website.
