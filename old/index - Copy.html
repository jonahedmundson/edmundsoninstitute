About

The Edmundson Institute is a brand under which I publish all of my creative and intellectual work. The goal of this project is to undercover truth, and to share my findings along the way. I am dedicated to ideas that promote human welfare and flourishing, primarily within the Christian tradition.

Everything you see was created by me, Jonah Edmundson. I have a BSc in Biology, a MSc in Data Science, and am working on my MA in English.
